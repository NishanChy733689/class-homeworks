Then when i inspected i found all the mangoes from our tree was gone and the poor tree was lying flat on the ground. I think someone wanted us to not have any more mangoes. The next day i wnet to Nishan's home then i saw alot of mangoes there. I asked him about it. He said that his dad brought this from the store.
